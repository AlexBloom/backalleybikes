Repair & Maintenance

The answers you seek begin with a visit.

Because we want to keep your bike under you and out of the shop as much as possible, service appointments are available, but in order to ensure availability of parts and sufficient time to complete all of our scheduled repairs, appointments must be made in person and with the bicycle. Diagnosis and service scheduling are available on a walk-in basis, so feel free to drop by with your bike anytime!

We love to ride and we’ve got the bikes and accessories to make your next ride legendary, but bicycle service is the living, breathing (drinking, smoking, cussing) soul of our business. We maintain and repair all makes, models, and styles of bicycles. From simple flat repairs and brake or derailleur adjustments to linkage rebuilds, suspension service, custom wheel builds, and drivetrain overhauls, we’ve got you covered.
